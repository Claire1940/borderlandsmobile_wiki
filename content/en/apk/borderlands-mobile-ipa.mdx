## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Even with the best hardware, the **borderlands mobile ipa** experience can occasionally run into hurdles. Here are the most common fixes for 2026:

### Crashing on Launch
This is usually caused by a lack of JIT or an improperly signed IPA. Ensure your AltStore or SideStore has "Enabled JIT" for the specific app session. On modern iOS versions, this may require a secondary device or a "JIT-enabler" shortcut.

### Missing Textures or Black Screens
This often indicates an issue with the GPU drivers within the emulator. If you are on Android, try switching between different Turnip drivers. For iOS users, ensure your device is not in "Low Power Mode," as this severely limits the Metal API's performance.

### Audio Crackling
Audio desync is a common side effect of the CPU falling behind the emulation speed. If you hear crackling, try lowering the resolution scale or disabling background apps to free up CPU cycles for the **borderlands mobile ipa** environment.
