## Gameplay Mechanics: Loot, Scrap, and Mods

One of the most surprising aspects of this mobile entry is that it introduces features fans have requested in the mainline games for years. The gameplay loop remains focused on the "shoot and loot" philosophy, but the management of that loot has been streamlined for mobile play.

### Weapon Scrapping and Power Levels
Unlike previous iterations where useless gear was simply sold for cash, this mobile game features a **Weapon Scrapping** system. You can break down unwanted firearms to receive "Scrap," which is then used to upgrade the power level of your favorite guns, grenades, and shields. This ensures that even a low-rarity weapon can stay relevant if you invest enough resources into it.

### The Modding System
The game features a dedicated weapon mod slotting system. Players can find mods throughout the world—such as fire rate boosters or reload speed stabilizers—and slot them directly into their weapons. These mods can also be leveled up independently, providing a deep layer of customization that exceeds the "Anointment" system found in Borderlands 3.

### Automated Features
To accommodate touchscreen controls, the game includes several "quality of life" automations:
*   **Auto-Loot:** Walking near ammo, money, or health packs automatically vacuums them into your inventory.
*   **Auto-Fire:** There is an optional toggle that allows your weapon to fire automatically when your reticle is over an enemy.
*   **Auto-Crate Opening:** Simply standing near a container will trigger it to open and scavenge the contents.

## The Sanctuary 3 Hub and AFK Rewards

The game uses **Sanctuary 3** as its central hub. From here, players can visit familiar locations like Moxxi’s Bar or Marcus’s Munitions. A unique addition to the mobile version is an "AFK" mechanic featuring the character Vaughn.

Vaughn will periodically head out on his own adventures while you are offline. When you log back in, you can claim "free loot" in the form of XP and cash that he collected during your absence. This is a standard mobile gaming feature designed to help players keep up with the level curve even if they only have a few minutes to play each day.
