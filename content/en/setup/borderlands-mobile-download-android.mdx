## Combat Tips for New Vault Hunters

Success in Borderlands Mobile requires more than just a fast trigger finger. You must understand the elemental rock-paper-scissors mechanic to take down tougher bosses like Shiv or the various Skag Queens.

*   **Incendiary (Fire)**: Highly effective against "flesh" (red health bars).
*   **Shock (Electric)**: Essential for stripping "shields" (blue health bars).
*   **Corrosive (Acid)**: The go-to element for "armor" (yellow health bars).
*   **Slag/Cryo**: Used to debuff or freeze enemies, making them take more damage from other sources.

Always keep a variety of elemental weapons in your inventory. Using a fire weapon against a shielded enemy is significantly less effective and will waste valuable ammunition.
