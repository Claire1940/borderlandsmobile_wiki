## Loot Boxes and the Battle Pass

Being a mobile title, the **borderlands mobile full release** incorporates several "live service" elements. The hub of the game is Sanctuary 3, where players can access various loot crates and the Battle Pass menu.

1.  **Common Loot Boxes:** These are free and operate on a 10-minute cooldown timer. They are excellent for gathering scrap early on.
2.  **Rarity Chests:** Rare, Epic, and Legendary chests require specific keys. These keys are earned by completing in-game challenges and achievements.
3.  **Battle Pass:** A tiered reward system is available at launch. While a free track exists, an "Ultimate Battle Pass" is currently in development and is expected to be a paid feature in future updates.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't hoard your keys early on. Using them to get Blue or Purple rarity gear at level 10-15 can significantly speed up your progression through the campaign missions.

## Controls and Optimization

The **borderlands mobile full release** features highly customizable controls. By default, the game utilizes an auto-fire mechanic—if your reticle is over an enemy, the gun fires automatically. This can be toggled off for players who prefer manual control.

*   **Movement:** Includes running and double-jumping. Note that sliding and mantling are currently absent from this version.
*   **Auto-Loot:** Money, ammo, and health are automatically picked up when walking near them.
*   **Controller Support:** The game is compatible with Bluetooth controllers, which is highly recommended for higher-level content like Raid Bosses where precision is key.
