## Core Mechanics: Mastering Phase Walk

Phase Walk is the cornerstone of Lilith's kit. When activated, Lilith enters a different dimension, becoming invisible to enemies and gaining a massive movement speed boost. This is not just a defensive tool; it is a versatile offensive ability that can trigger powerful elemental explosions upon entry and exit.

To maximize the effectiveness of Phase Walk, players should focus on skills that reduce cooldown and increase duration. The "Hit and Run" skill is particularly vital, as it extends the time you spend in Phase Walk, allowing you to reposition or wait for your shields to recharge.

| Skill Name | Tree | Primary Benefit | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hit and Run** | Assassin | Increases Phase Walk duration | Essential for survivability and mobility. |
| **Hard to Hit** | Defender | Increases Shield Capacity | Provides a larger buffer for when Phase Walk is on cooldown. |
| **Inner Glow** | Defender | Health Regeneration during Phase Walk | Turns your action skill into a portable medkit. |
| **Spark** | Elemental | Increases Elemental Proc Chance | Synergizes perfectly with Lilith's preferred SMGs. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always carry an elemental artifact. Swapping to a Fire or Corrosive artifact before entering a group of enemies allows your Phase Walk blast to deal massive Area of Effect (AoE) damage.

This build focuses on the "Phoenix" skill, which gives you a chance to not consume ammo and deals constant fire damage to nearby enemies after a kill. It is widely considered the highest DPS build for Lilith in 2026.

Lilith is famously proficient with Submachine Guns (SMGs). Because of her "Mercenary" class mod, she can achieve near-infinite ammo regeneration and massive damage boosts with this weapon type. However, a well-rounded Siren should also keep high-damage revolvers and snipers in her inventory.

| Weapon Name | Type | Key Feature | Best Used For |

## Farming Strategies for 2026

To reach the peak of Siren performance, you will need to farm specific locations for "on-level" gear. The mobile version of Borderlands retains the classic loot tables found in the [Official Borderlands Game Site](https://borderlands.2k.com/), meaning veteran strategies still apply.

### The Hyperion Gift Shop
After completing the Claptrap New Robot Revolution DLC, you gain access to the Hyperion Gift Shop. This area contains several red chests that do not require any combat to loot. It is the most efficient way to find high-quality Class Mods, such as the **Plague Bearer** (for corrosive builds) or the **Mercenary** (for SMG builds).

### The Zombie Island of Dr. Ned
The Zombie Island is an excellent place to farm for "Action Skill" and "Melee" challenges. Because zombies often swarm in large groups, you can use a Fire Artifact with Phase Walk to obliterate dozens of enemies at once. This is also the best place to farm the "Brains" mission for TK Baja, which provides significant XP and loot rewards.

> **⚠️ Warning:** When farming the Zombie Island, ensure you have accepted the "Brains" mission from TK Baja before you start killing. Brains dropped by zombies will not count toward the mission progress until the quest is active in your log.

## Multiplayer and Achievement Hunting

If you are looking to complete 100% of the game on mobile, you will likely encounter achievements that require multiplayer participation. One of the more tedious achievements involves completing 12 missions with at least one other player.

To finish this quickly:
1.  **Use the Rust Commons:** This area has a high density of short, fetch-style missions.
2.  **Join Community Discords:** Look for "Looking For Group" (LFG) channels specifically for the mobile or remastered versions of the game.
3.  **Speed Run:** Have a high-level Lilith host the game and use Phase Walk to reach objectives instantly while the other player stays at the fast-travel station.

## Advanced Combat Tactics

When facing high-tier bosses like Crawmerax the Invincible or General Knoxx, Lilith's strategy shifts from aggressive SMG fire to careful Phase Walk management.

1.  **Shield Stripping:** Use a shock-based weapon or the "Radiance" skill to remove shields.
2.  **The "Exit Blast":** When exiting Phase Walk, stand as close to the enemy as possible to trigger the elemental explosion.
3.  **Weapon Proficiencies:** Stick to one or two weapon types (ideally SMGs and Revolvers) to maximize your Proficiency levels, which grant passive bonuses to damage and reload speed.

| Proficiency Level | Bonus Type | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Level 10** | Accuracy/Reload | Noticeable reduction in sway. |
| **Level 25** | Damage/Fire Rate | Significant DPS increase for endgame. |
| **Level 50** | Master Level | Massive boosts that make legendary weapons trivial. |

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best class mod for Lilith in the Borderlands mobile version?**

A: The **Mercenary** class mod is widely considered the best because it provides SMG ammo regeneration and increases SMG damage. If you are running a corrosive build for the Secret Armory DLC, the **Plague Bearer** is a strong alternative.

**Q: How do I farm Action Skill kills quickly for challenges?**

A: Head to the Zombie Island of Dr. Ned. Equip a Fire Artifact and use Phase Walk to run into a cluster of zombies. The fire blast upon entering and exiting will count as Action Skill kills, allowing you to finish the challenge in a fraction of the time.

**Q: Is Lilith viable for solo play on mobile?**

A: Yes, Lilith is arguably the best solo character. Her Phase Walk allows her to escape dangerous situations and regenerate health, making her much more self-sufficient than Roland or Mordecai.

**Q: Where can I find a Double Anarchy SMG?**

A: The Double Anarchy is a random drop, but it frequently appears in Marcus's vending machines and the Hyperion Gift Shop chests. Look for SMGs with the "Double" prefix and a "x4" damage multiplier in this **borderlands mobile lilith guide**.
