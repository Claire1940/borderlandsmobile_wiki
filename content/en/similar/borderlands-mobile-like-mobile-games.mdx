## Understanding the Gear System

The gear system in the official mobile game takes inspiration from modern ARPGs and MMO-lites like *Destiny 2*. Instead of just looking at raw damage, players must manage their "Gear Power." This score acts as a gatekeeper for harder content. If your Gear Power is significantly lower than the recommended level for a raid, you will deal less damage and take significantly more.

### Weapon Scrapping and Modding
The introduction of weapon scrapping is a game-changer for the series. Instead of selling thousands of "trash" guns to a vending machine for a pittance of currency, you can now dismantle them. This yields "Weapon Essence" and "Component Scraps."

These materials are used in the new Mod Slotting system. Unlike the "Anointment" system in *Borderlands 3*, which was largely RNG-dependent, the mobile game allows you to find specific mods (e.g., +20% Fire Damage, +10% Magazine Size) and slot them into your favorite guns. This makes the hunt for the "perfect roll" much more interactive and rewarding.
