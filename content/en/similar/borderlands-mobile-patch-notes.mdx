## Borderlands 4 Weekly Hotfix: The Combat XP Overhaul

While the mobile game takes the spotlight for many, the mainline series received a significant hotfix this week. The core focus of this update is a massive increase in combat experience gained from defeating high-tier enemies. For those tracking the **borderlands mobile patch notes** and general franchise updates, this change is a direct response to players feeling that the grind to the level cap was becoming stagnant.

## Optimal Leveling: The Timekeeper Method

With the new XP buffs in place, players are searching for the most efficient path to max level. The **borderlands mobile patch notes** suggest that while most bosses now give double XP, certain encounters are mathematically superior. The "Timekeeper" boss encounter stands out because the game effectively counts it as two boss kills within a single session.

If your character build has high burst damage, the Timekeeper method is currently the fastest way to gain levels. You only need to defeat the Timekeeper roughly three times to equal the XP output of a single Vile Bounty. Because this method removes the need for constant loading screens and traveling to different bounty boards, the "XP per hour" is significantly higher than traditional methods.

1.  **Travel to the Timekeeper arena.**
2.  **Deploy high-burst DPS skills** to clear the first phase quickly.
3.  **Defeat both entities** to trigger the double boss XP payout.
4.  **Save and Quit** to reset the encounter instantly.

## Maurice’s Black Market: Location & Inventory

Maurice has moved his Black Market Vending Machine once again. For the week of April 14, 2026, you will need to head to the planet of Windspear to find him. His current inventory is somewhat specialized, featuring items that focus on utility and spiked damage reflect.

**Location Steps:**
1.  Fast travel to the **Night Cap Safe House** in Windspear.
2.  Exit the safe house and head left toward the **Vile Prototype** arena.
3.  Locate the elevator shaft inside the facility and drop down to the bottom.
4.  Search the area directly underneath the elevator for a **power canister**.
5.  Carry the canister to the nearby locked door and insert it into the slot.
6.  The door will cycle open, revealing Maurice's hidden shop.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the new mobile game require a Borderlands 4 save file?**

A: No, the Zynga-developed mobile game is a standalone experience. While it shares lore and assets from the main series, your progress in Borderlands 4 does not currently carry over to the mobile version.

**Q: Are the XP buffs in the borderlands mobile patch notes permanent?**

A: These changes were implemented via a hotfix. While hotfixes are usually permanent adjustments to the game's balance, Gearbox occasionally tweaks these numbers in future "Title Updates" based on player data and leveling speed.

**Q: Can I play the mobile game on Android and iOS?**

A: As of the initial shadow drop, the game is rolling out in phases. It is currently available in select regions on iOS and Android, with a global rollout expected to conclude by the end of April 2026.

**Q: Who is the best boss to farm for XP after the latest update?**

A: For raw efficiency, the Timekeeper is the best choice due to the double-boss XP bug/feature. However, if you prefer a more varied gameplay loop, Drill Sites now offer a comparable XP-to-time ratio to Vile Bounties.
