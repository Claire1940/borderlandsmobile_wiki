## Navigating the Psycho Lands and Tower of Terror

Endgame content in Borderlands Mobile is divided into several high-stakes modes. The Psycho Lands serve as an open-world farming zone, while the Tower of Terror offers a roguelike experience where your build is tested floor by floor. 

To survive the later stages of the Tower of Terror, your **borderlands mobile best build 2026** must include some form of health sustain. Since health vials are scarce in these modes, utilizing the "Elemental Siphon" mod on a high-fire-rate SMG or Pistol is the most reliable way to stay in the fight.

### Endgame Activity Comparison

1. **Circle of Slaughter:** Wave-based combat that rewards massive amounts of XP and weapon components.
2. **Tower of Terror:** A vertical progression mode that drops exclusive weapon mods and cosmetic rewards.
3. **Raid Bosses:** Massive encounters requiring high Power Level gear; these are the primary source of Legendary mods.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt Raid Bosses if your total Power Level is more than 10 points below the recommended threshold. The damage penalty is severe and will likely result in a quick defeat.
