## The Summoner: Skills and Progression

The Summoner is the standout star of the early 2026 gameplay sessions. Her kit is built around three distinct specialization paths that allow players to tailor their **borderlands mobile game vault hunters** experience to their preferred combat rhythm. 

### Core Skill Paths
1.  **Summon Durability:** Focuses on the health and damage output of your pet Skag. This is essential for players who prefer a "tank and spank" approach where the pet draws aggro.
2.  **Raw Gun Damage:** A path dedicated to traditional Borderlands gunplay, boosting critical hit damage and magazine size.
3.  **Elemental Efficiency:** Enhances the effects of Incendiary, Shock, and Corrosive damage, allowing the Summoner to melt through shielded or armored enemies quickly.

> **💡 Tip:** In the early game, invest your first skill points into pet health. Having a reliable companion to distract enemies makes the transition to mobile touch controls much smoother.

The progression system is tied directly to experience points earned through mission completion. As your level increases, you unlock higher-tier gear slots and more complex nodes in the skill tree. Interestingly, the mobile version introduces a "Scrap" mechanic where unwanted loot can be dismantled to provide materials for upgrading your favorite legendary weapons.

## The Hub Ship and Meta-Progression

Between missions, your **borderlands mobile game vault hunters** reside on a central Hub Ship. This area serves as your primary menu and base of operations. The ship itself is upgradable, and as you improve its systems, you unlock better rewards and more efficient meta-progression.

### Key Hub Ship Functions
*   **Gear Management:** Equip, scrap, and upgrade weapons, shields, and grenades.
*   **The Chest Location:** Use keys earned from missions to open Rare, Epic, and Legendary chests. The "Red Chest" resets periodically, providing a steady stream of basic loot.
*   **Mission Selection:** Access the story campaign, side missions, and the Tower of Terror.
*   **Daily Challenges:** A list of rotating objectives that provide currency and upgrade materials.

## End-Game Content: Tower of Terror and Circle of Slaughter

For those who have reached the level cap during the 2026 technical test, the game offers several high-stakes modes to test your build. The **Tower of Terror** is a 25-floor gauntlet where players must spend tickets to enter and climb as high as possible for escalating rewards.

The **Circle of Slaughter** remains a fan favorite, tasking players with surviving waves of increasingly difficult enemies. These arenas are the primary source for legendary gear, such as the Star Helix or the Lob. Success in these modes requires a deep understanding of elemental weaknesses and a highly optimized skill tree.

> **Warning:** High-level floors in the Tower of Terror feature aggressive level scaling. Ensure your weapon level matches the floor's recommended power before spending your entry tickets.
