## Technical Performance and Requirements

Since this is a beta/alpha build, the technical requirements are subject to change. Currently, the game features "PC-level" graphics, utilizing high-quality cel-shading that mimics the look of *Borderlands 3*. However, this visual fidelity comes at a cost. The game currently has very limited settings:
*   **No Audio Sliders:** Players cannot currently adjust music vs. SFX volumes.
*   **Static HUD:** You cannot move the on-screen buttons, which may be difficult for players who prefer a "four-finger claw" grip.
*   **Resolution Scaling:** The game performs well on modern iPads but may see some resolution drops on older iPhone models to maintain a steady frame rate.
