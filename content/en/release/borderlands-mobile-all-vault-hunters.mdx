## Skill Trees and Meta Progression

The skill tree system has been reimagined for shorter, mobile-friendly sessions. While it retains the core depth of the franchise, the paths are more focused. When looking at **borderlands mobile all vault hunters**, each character features three distinct specialization paths.

For the Summoner, these paths are:
1.  **Summon Durability**: Focuses on keeping your pet alive and buffing its health and armor.
2.  **Raw Gun Damage**: Enhances the Vault Hunter's personal lethality with weapon-specific buffs.
3.  **Elemental Efficiency**: Increases the proc rate and damage of Incendiary, Shock, and Corrosive statuses.

> **💡 Tip:** Even if you prefer solo play, investing at least a few points into the Summoner's pet durability is highly recommended for the higher floors of the Tower of Terror.

## The Hub Ship: Your Central Command

Unlike the expansive open worlds of Pandora or Promethea, Borderlands Mobile utilizes a mission-based structure centered around the **Hub Ship**. This area serves as your primary menu for gear management, meta progression, and mission selection. It is designed to be highly addictive, offering a streamlined "loop" of killing, looting, and upgrading.

### Upgrading Your Loadout
The Hub Ship is where you will spend the majority of your time between missions. The progression system is "aggressive," meaning you are constantly encouraged to scrap old gear to improve your current kit.

| Upgrade Category | Required Currency | Primary Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Weapons** | Cash + Weapon Scraps | Increases Base Damage |
| **Shields** | Cash + Shield Components | Boosts Capacity & Recharge Rate |
| **Grenades** | Cash + Explosive Residue | Expands Blast Radius & Damage |

To stay competitive, you must manually scrap gear you don't want. This provides the necessary materials to level up your Legendary items. The rarity system remains familiar, ranging from White (Common) to Orange (Legendary), with Legendaries being obtainable through specific events like the Circle of Slaughter or daily login rewards.

## Combat and Game Modes

The combat in Borderlands Mobile maintains the signature fast-paced, first-person gunplay the series is known for. While touch controls are the default, the game features full Bluetooth controller support. Using a PlayStation 5 or Xbox controller is highly recommended for precision, especially during the 25-floor **Tower of Terror** challenge.

### Available Game Modes in 2026
*   **Story Campaign**: Mission-based chapters that follow the fall of the Calypso Twins and the rise of corporate tyranny.
*   **Side Missions**: Contained encounters designed for 5-10 minute play sessions.
*   **Tower of Terror**: A 25-floor gauntlet where players must spend tickets to climb higher for better rewards.
*   **Circle of Slaughter**: A wave-based arena mode perfect for farming high-tier gear like the Legendary Star Helix.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Microtransactions are currently disabled in the technical test, but a Premium Battle Pass and paid currency are expected in the full global release.

## Gear Brands and Elemental Damage

True to the franchise's roots, the weapon pool includes pistols, shotguns, SMGs, assault rifles, sniper rifles, and rocket launchers. Each manufacturer retains its unique identity. For example, Tediore weapons still feature the "reload explosion" mechanic, where the gun is thrown like a grenade when you reload.

Elemental damage remains a cornerstone of the strategy. You will need to swap between different types to effectively take down shielded or armored enemies:
*   **Incendiary**: Massive damage against flesh (red health bars).
*   **Shock**: Quickly strips enemy shields (blue health bars).
*   **Corrosive**: Dissolves armor (yellow health bars).

## FAQ

**Q: Who are the borderlands mobile all vault hunters available at launch?**

A: The current roster includes the Summoner (playable now), the Exotank, the Outrider, and the Assassin. While only the Summoner is accessible during the initial technical test, the other three are scheduled for release in upcoming updates.

A: No, this title uses a mission-based structure. Instead of one seamless world, players operate from a Hub Ship and deploy into contained, high-action environments for specific objectives.
