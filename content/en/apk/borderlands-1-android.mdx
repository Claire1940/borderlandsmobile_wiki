## Optimizing Performance for Mobile Devices

If you find that your device is heating up or the frame rate is dropping during heavy combat, consider the following optimizations:

1.  **Reduce Shadows:** Shadow rendering is one of the most taxing processes for mobile GPUs. Set this to "Low" for a significant FPS boost.
2.  **Limit Frame Rate:** If your phone supports 120Hz but can't maintain it, lock the game to 60 FPS to prevent thermal throttling.
3.  **Disable Bloom:** While it makes the explosions look pretty, it can clutter the small screen and impact performance.
4.  **Use Game Mode:** Most modern Android phones (Samsung, ASUS, Xiaomi) have a built-in Game Mode that clears background RAM and prioritizes CPU cycles for the game.
