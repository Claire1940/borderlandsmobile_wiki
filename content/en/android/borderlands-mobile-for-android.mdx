## Understanding the Loot and Gear System

Loot is the heartbeat of any Borderlands game, and this mobile iteration is no different. The game utilizes a rarity system ranging from Common (White) to Legendary (Orange). ## Progression and the "Vaughn AFK" System

A unique addition to this mobile entry is the inclusion of Vaughn, who manages an "AFK" loot system. While you are away from the game, Vaughn "collects" XP and money on your behalf. This ensures that even casual players who can only log in for a few minutes a day can keep up with the power curve. You can claim these rewards once every few hours to help fund your gear upgrades and skill point investments.

The skill tree remains the backbone of your build. While it is less complex than the multi-branching trees of the mainline games, it offers essential buffs to health, reload speed, and elemental damage. By level 18, most players will have a clear "build" established, focusing on either the Siren's personal damage or Nigel's survivability.
