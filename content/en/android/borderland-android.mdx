## Troubleshooting Common Mobile Issues

Even with a powerful device, you might encounter issues during the early days of the **borderland android** release. Optimization for the vast array of Android hardware is a complex task for any developer.

*   **Frequent Crashes:** This is often caused by memory leaks. Try closing all background applications before launching the game.
*   **Overheating:** Lowering the frame rate cap from 60 FPS to 30 FPS can significantly reduce the heat generated by your GPU.
*   **Connection Errors:** As an always-online looter-shooter, a stable Wi-Fi connection is preferred over cellular data to prevent "rubber-banding" during combat.
