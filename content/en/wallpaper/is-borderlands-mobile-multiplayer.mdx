## Gameplay Mechanics and Progression

The 2026 mobile version introduces several quality-of-life features that fans have requested for years in the mainline series. These mechanics change the way players interact with loot and character builds.

### Weapon Scrapping and Modding
In this version, players can finally scrap unwanted gear directly for resources. Instead of just selling items for cash, you receive "Scrap," which is used to upgrade the power levels of your favorite guns, grenades, and shields. This is a significant departure from the RNG-heavy systems of the past, allowing for more consistent progression.

Furthermore, the game features a dedicated **Weapon Mod Slotting** system. Instead of relying on random anointments, you can find or earn mods—such as fire rate increases or reload speed buffs—and manually slot them into your weapons. These mods can also be leveled up independently, providing a deep layer of customization for your arsenal.

### Gear and Rarity
The loot system follows the classic rarity tiers, ranging from Common (White) to Legendary (Orange). The game also implements a "Power Level" system, similar to modern looter-shooters like *Destiny 2*, which determines your eligibility for harder content like the Tower of Terror.
