## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Even with the best setup, you might encounter technical hurdles. Here are the most common fixes for 2026 emulation:

*   **Black Screen on Launch**: Update your graphics card drivers. Emulators often require the latest OpenGL or Vulkan drivers to render mobile games correctly.
*   **Laggy Input**: Check if your "Polling Rate" on your gaming mouse is too high. Sometimes setting it to 500Hz instead of 1000Hz helps emulators process the movement better.
*   **Game Crashes**: Ensure you are using the "DirectX" rendering mode in the emulator settings if "OpenGL" fails, as some Unreal Engine titles prefer the Windows-native API.
