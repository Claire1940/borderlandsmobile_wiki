One of the most surprising additions is the **Weapon Scrapping** system. In this mobile iteration, players are no longer forced to simply leave loot on the ground or sell it for meager amounts of cash. Instead, you can scrap unwanted gear to obtain resources for upgrading your primary loadout.

## Technical Optimization and Visuals

To ensure the game runs smoothly on mobile devices, Zynga has implemented significant technical optimizations. While the game looks remarkably like its PC and console counterparts at a glance, several "under the hood" changes have been made:

1.  **Texture Resolution:** Most textures have been downscaled to 256 or 512 resolutions to save on VRAM.
2.  **Model Complexity:** Character and boss models use lower polygon counts, though the signature "cel-shaded" art style hides this effectively.
3.  **Audio Assets:** Interestingly, the game reuses sound effects from the original *Borderlands 1*, providing a nostalgic feel for veteran Vault Hunters.

These optimizations allow the game to maintain a high level of performance even during chaotic combat sequences with multiple elemental effects on screen.
