## Troubleshooting Common Download Errors

Even with a high-speed connection, you might encounter issues when you try to **download borderlands mobile**. Most errors are related to server congestion or local storage permissions.

1.  **"App Not Compatible" Error:** This usually means your device's GPU does not support the required Vulkan API version. Ensure your firmware is updated to the latest 2026 security patch.
2.  **Download Stuck at 99%:** This is often a verification error. Restart your device and ensure you have at least 5 GB of extra "buffer" space beyond the game's actual size.
3.  **Network Timeout:** Toggle Airplane Mode on and off to reset your DNS settings, or try switching from 5G to a stable Wi-Fi 6 connection.

| Error Code | Meaning | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ERR_104** | Authentication Failure | Re-login to your App Store account. |
| **ERR_STORAGE** | Insufficient Space | Delete unused apps and retry the download. |
| **ERR_VULKAN** | Graphics Incompatibility | Check for system updates or lower graphics settings. |
