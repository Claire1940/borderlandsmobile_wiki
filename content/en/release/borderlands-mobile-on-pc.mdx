## Gameplay Mechanics and Progression

The mobile version introduces a "Power Level" system that is far more functional than the Gear Score seen in *Borderlands 3*. Players must upgrade their gear's power level to access harder difficulty tiers, similar to the masterworking systems found in other major live-service titles.

### Weapon Scrapping and Modding
Instead of just selling loot to Marcus, you can now scrap items for specific currency used to upgrade your favorite guns. This ensures that even "bad" drops have value. Furthermore, the mod slotting system allows you to add fire rate, reload speed, or elemental damage mods to your weapons. These mods can also be leveled up independently, creating a deep layer of progression for dedicated players.

### Playable Characters and Skills
At the start of the 2026 regional test, only one character is playable—a Siren with a focus on pet-based combat. While her skill tree is currently simplified compared to mainline entries, it offers essential stat boosts like increased HP and faster action skill cooldowns. Zynga has confirmed that three additional characters are in development, each with unique action skills and tactical niches.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The game currently features a "Battle Pass" system. While it offers free rewards, the "Ultimate" tier will likely require real-money transactions. Always monitor your spending on microtransactions within free-to-play mobile environments.
