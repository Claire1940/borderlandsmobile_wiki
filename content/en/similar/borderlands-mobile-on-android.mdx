## Combat and Touch Controls

The transition to mobile brings a new way to interact with the world of Pandora. The developers have implemented a highly customizable HUD that allows you to move buttons to fit your grip. Optimizing your layout for **borderlands mobile on android** is crucial for surviving late-game encounters where movement is just as important as accuracy.

### Mastering the HUD

*   **Auto-Fire Options**: For beginners, the auto-fire setting can help manage the chaos. It triggers your weapon whenever your reticle passes over a valid target.
*   **Gyro Aiming**: 2026 has seen a massive improvement in gyroscope sensors. Enabling gyro aiming allows for micro-adjustments by physically tilting your phone, which is often more precise than thumb-sliding.
*   **Controller Support**: If you find touch controls difficult, the game natively supports most Bluetooth controllers. This is often the preferred way to play for long sessions.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when using third-party "overlay" apps for remapping buttons, as some anti-cheat systems may flag these as unauthorized software. Always stick to the in-game customization menu.
