## Gameplay Mechanics and Loot System

The core loop of the game remains true to the series: shoot enemies, collect loot, and upgrade your Vault Hunter. However, there are some significant "quality of life" changes designed specifically for mobile players.

### Automated Features
To make the game more accessible on touchscreens, several features have been automated:
*   **Auto-Loot:** Running over ammo, money, and health vials automatically adds them to your inventory.
*   **Auto-Fire:** If your reticle is over an enemy, the gun will fire automatically (this can be toggled off in the settings for a more traditional experience).
*   **Scrap System:** You can "scrap" unwanted gear directly from your inventory to gain resources for upgrading your favorite weapons.

### Mission Types
Missions are divided into several categories to allow for "bite-sized" gaming sessions:
*   **Campaign Missions:** Short, 5-minute segments of the main story.
*   **Tower of Terror:** A mobbing encounter where you collect ship parts under a time limit.
*   **Bounty Hunts:** Specialized boss fights that reward high-tier loot crates.
*   **Vaughn’s AFK Quest:** A unique feature where Vaughn collects XP and money for you while you are offline.

> **💡 Tip:** Check in with Vaughn every few hours. His AFK rewards cap out, so you need to claim them frequently to maximize your resource gain.
