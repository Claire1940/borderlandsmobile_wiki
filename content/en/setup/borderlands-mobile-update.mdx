> **💡 Tip:** Do not hoard low-level white and green gear. Scrap them immediately to funnel resources into your first Blue or Purple weapon to speed up your early-game progression.

## Quality of Life and Controls

Adapting a fast-paced shooter to a touchscreen is always a challenge, but this mobile entry includes several features to make the transition easier.

*   **Auto-Loot:** Money, ammo, and health are automatically picked up when you walk near them.
*   **Auto-Shoot:** A toggleable feature that automatically fires your weapon when your crosshair is over an enemy.
*   **Compass System:** A HUD element in the upper right corner that tracks enemy positions, making it easier to navigate small mobile screens.
*   **Controller Support:** For those who prefer a traditional experience, the game supports Bluetooth controllers, which is highly recommended for higher-level Raid Bosses.

### Movement Limitations
It is worth noting that some movement mechanics from Borderlands 3 have been removed to simplify the mobile control scheme. There is currently **no sliding** and **no mantling**. The focus is strictly on running, double jumping, and positioning.
